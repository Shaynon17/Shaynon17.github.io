Easy Rules

Objective

- Each player starts with 5 unloaded bullets.
- The first player to have all 10 bullets wins.
- Bullets represent your score, and get exchanged between players as the game progresses.

Reload

- Reloading removes a bullet from your inventory and reloads you.
- You may only have one bullet loaded at a time.

Shoot

- You must have a bullet loaded to shoot successfully, otherwise you will shoot a blank.
- After you shoot, the bullet goes back to your inventory.
- If you shoot someone who is reloading, they still reload, and you get a bullet from their inventory.
- If you shoot someone who is trying to punch, you get a bullet from their inventory.
- If both players shoot, the bullets cancel each other out, and go back to each players inventory.

Block

- Blocking only blocks bullets.

Punch

- If you punch someone who is blocking, you take a bullet from their inventory.
- If you punch someone who is reloading, you cancel their reload.

Normal Rules

Along with the easy rules, each player starts loaded, and you may not punch twice in a row.
