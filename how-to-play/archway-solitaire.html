How to Play Archway Solitaire

Archway Solitaire Rules

| Players: 1 | Type: Solitaire | Supplies: 2 Decks |

Objective

The objective of Archway is to build the four ace foundation piles up to the kings in the same suit, and to build the four king foundation piles down to the aces in the same suit.

Setup

Take an ace of each suit and a king of each suit out of the deck and place them at the bottom of the layout to form the eight foundation piles. Above the foundation piles, deal four central columns of twelve cards each, face up, splayed down.

The remaining cards in the deck are organized by ranks into 13 reserve piles that form an arch (a semi-circle) around the four columns. Each of the 13 reserve piles must consist of cards of only one rank, thus creating a reserve pile for each rank from ace to king. For example, all remaining aces go in the ace reserve pile, all remaining 2s go in the 2 reserve pile, and so on. Some reserve piles may be empty. Position the reserve piles of aces at the bottom left of the arch, the reserve pile of 7s at the top in the middle, and the reserve pile of kings at the bottom right. The rest of the reserve piles should be placed in sequential order based on rank between these piles.

Game Play

You may only place cards on the ace foundation piles if they are the next highest card in rank and of the same suit. You may only place cards on the king foundation piles if they are the next lowest rank and of the same suit. You may use any card from any reserve pile and place it on the foundation piles at any time; you are not limited to the top card of the reserve piles and may select any needed card.

You may only play the top card from any of the four central piles into the foundation piles. Once the top card is taken, the next card becomes available to play. When one of these four central piles runs out of cards, you may move the top card from another central pile to this position.

To Win

Archway is won by having all four ace foundation piles built in ascending sequence and suit to the king, and all four king foundation piles built in descending sequence and suit to the ace. If no more moves are available and the foundation piles are not complete, the game is lost.
