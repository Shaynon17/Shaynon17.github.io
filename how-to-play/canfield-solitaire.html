How to Play Canfield Solitaire

Canfield Solitaire solitaire is a challenging solitaire game where the foundation piles start cards are not determined until flipping a card.

Canfield Solitaire Rules

| Players: 1 | Type: Solitaire | Supplies: 1 Deck |

Objective

The objective of Canfield is to move all the cards onto the foundation piles.

Setup

Deal 13 cards face down to form the reserve pile. Flip the top card of the reserve pile face-up. Deal a row of four cards face up to form the four tableau piles. Deal one face-up card above the tableau piles to form the first foundation pile. Place the remaining deck face down to form the stock.

Game Play

There will be a total of four foundation piles, one per suit. The first foundation pile that was started during setup, determines the starting rank of the other three foundation piles. When cards of the same starting rank become available, move them above the tableau to form another foundation pile. Foundation piles are built up in sequence and suit, until each pile contains 13 cards. Aces may be played on kings to continue the sequence.

The top card of each tableau pile, the reserve pile, and the waste pile are available for play onto a foundation pile or a tableau pile. Tableau piles may be built down in sequence and alternating color. Kings may be played on aces to continue the sequence. A card or group of cards in the correct sequence may be moved from one tableau pile to another as long as the correct sequence continues. When a tableau pile becomes empty, it must be filled with the top card of the reserve pile. If the reserve pile is empty, then an empty tableau pile may be filled using any other available card.

When there are no more available moves the player wants to make, flip the top three cards of the stock over to form or add to the waste pile. When the stock is depleted, you may redeal by flipping the waste pile over to form a new stock. You may redeal as many times as you want.

To Win

Canfield is won if all four foundation piles are successfully built up to 13 cards.
