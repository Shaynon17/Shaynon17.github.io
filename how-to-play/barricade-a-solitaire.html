How to Play Barricade A Solitaire

Barricade A solitaire is a solitaire game that uses two decks of playing cards.

Barricade A Solitaire Rules

| Players: 1 | Type: Solitaire | Supplies: 2 Decks |

Objective

Barricade A solitaire is a solitaire game that uses two decks of playing cards.

Setup

Deal out 12 tableau piles with three face up cards in each pile. Place the remaining deck face up to form the stock.

Game Play

The top card of each tableau pile and stock are available for play. When an ace becomes available it is moved to form a foundation pile. Foundation piles are built from ace to king regardless of suit. To add a card to a foundation pile, it must be one rank higher than the card it is being played on. No foundation pile may have more than 13 cards.

Only one card may be moved from the stock pile or a tableau pile to a tableau pile or a foundation pile. Tableau piles are built down in sequence regardless of suit. When a tableau pile becomes empty, it is filled by the top card of the stock, if there are no more cards in the stock, it may be filled by any available card.

To Win

To win barricade a solitaire, all eight foundation piles must be built from ace to king.
