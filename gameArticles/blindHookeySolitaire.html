How to Play Blind Hookey Solitaire

Blind hookey solitaire is a two deck solitaire game with few rules.

Blind Hookey Solitaire Rules

| Players: 1 | Type: Solitaire | Supplies: 2 Decks |

Objective

The objective of blind hookey solitaire is to build the foundations up in sequence from ace to king in suit.

Setup

Deal out ten tableau piles with four cards each, only the top card is face up, the rest are face down. Place the remaining deck face down to form the stock.

Game Play

When an ace becomes available, move it above the tableau to form a foundation pile. Foundation piles are built from ace to king, in suit. Only 13 cards per foundation pile are allowed.

The top card of each tableau pile and the waste pile are available for play. Only one card may be moved at a time. Tableau piles may be built down in sequence and alternating color. When a tableau pile is empty, it may be filled by any available card.

To Win

To win blind hookey solitaire, all cards must be built in the eight foundation piles, from ace to king in suit.
