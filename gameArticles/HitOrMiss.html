How to Play Hit or Miss Card Game

Hit or miss is one of the fastest solitaire games to learn and play. Hit or miss uses one deck of playing cards and does not take up much space.

Hit or Miss Card Game Rules

| Players: 1 | Type: Solitaire | Supplies: 1 Deck |

Objective

The objective of hit or miss is to discard cards that match what you are saying as you count out loud.

Setup

Shuffle the cards and place the entire deck face down.

Card Rank

(Lowest Rank) Ace, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9, 10, Jack, Queen, King (Highest Rank)

Game Play

Deal one card at a time into a waste pile, as you count the card sequence ace to king out loud. As you deal the first card, you say “ace”, deal the second card and say “two”, deal the third card and say “three,” and so on. This sequence continues all the way to dealing the 13th card when you say “King”. After you say king you start over again at ace.

When you deal a card that matches what you have said, that card is discarded, and you continue to deal while continuing to say the sequence where you left off. When all cards have been dealt, as long as you have discarded at least one card (known as a hit) you can redeal the cards, and pick up the counting sequence where you left off. If you redeal the cards two times in a row without discarding a card, you lose.

How to Win Hit or Miss

If all cards are discarded, hit or miss is won. If you redeal two times in a row without discarding a card, hit or miss is lost.

Variants

-Frustration is a similar game that is typically much faster to play.
