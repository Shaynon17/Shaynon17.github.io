How to Play Double Nestor Solitaire

Double nestor solitaire is a two deck variation of nestor solitaire. Double nestor Solitaire is an easy to learn and quick solitaire game.

Double Nestor Solitaire Rules

| Players: 1 | Type: Solitaire | Supplies: 2 Decks |

Objective

The objective of double nestor solitaire is to discard all cards in pairs of matching rank.

Setup

Deal out 10 tableau piles with 10 face up cards each, splayed down, Place the remaining four cards face up in a row to form the reserve.

Game Play

Only the top card of each tableau pile, and each card in the reserve are available for play. When two available cards match in rank, discard them. Game play continues until there are no more available matches.

There is no redeal and empty tableau spaces are not filled.

To Win

To win double nestor solitaire, all cards must be discarded in pairs.
